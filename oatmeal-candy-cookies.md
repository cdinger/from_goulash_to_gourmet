---
title: Oatmeal Candy Cookies
categories: Candy
---

## Ingredients

- 2 C. sugar
- ½ C. peanut butter
- 4 T. cocoa
- 3 C. quick oats
- ½ C. milk
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- ½ C. butter

## Preparation

Boil sugar, cocoa, milk and butter for 4 to 5 minutes.  Remove from heat and add peanut butter, oats and vanilla.  Mix well and drop by spoon on wax paper.
