## Ingredients

- Basil
- Sage
- Oregano
- Rosemary
- Cracked pepper
- Dried red pepper
- 1 tsp. Minced garlic
- Salt
- Soy sauce
- 2 tsp. honey
- Red wine vinegar

## Preparation

Mix amounts of each ingredient (to suit tastes) and make paste.  Rub paste on pork roast and let marinate before baking.
