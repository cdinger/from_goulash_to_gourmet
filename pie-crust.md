---
title: Pie Crust
categories: Pies
---

## Ingredients

- 3 C. flour
- 1 large egg
- 1 ½ tsp. salt
- 1 T vinegar
- 1 ¼ C. lard or 1 ½ C. shortening
- 5 T water

## Preparation

Sift, then measure flour, salt and lard.  Mix well with fork.  Beat egg well. Combine vinegar and water.  Add to egg mixture, beating well.  Add to flour mixture.  Makes 4 crusts.
