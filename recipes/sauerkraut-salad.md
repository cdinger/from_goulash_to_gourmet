---
title: Sauerkraut Salad
categories: Salads
---

## Ingredients

- 1 can bean sprouts
- 1 small jar pimento
- 3 ½ C. sauerkraut, drained
- 1 C. sugar (or less)
- ¾ C. green pepper
- 4 T sauerkraut juice
- ¾ C. onion
- ¼ C. vinegar
- ¾ C. celery

## Preparation

Mix sugar, vinegar, and juice and boil 3 min.  Cool and pour over veggies.  Toss and chill for 24 hours. (Red pepper can be used for pimento.)
