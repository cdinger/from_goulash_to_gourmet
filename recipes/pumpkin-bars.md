title: Pumpkin Bars
categories: Bars
---

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1 tsp soda
- 1 C. oil
- ½ tsp salt
- 2 C. sugar
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 1-15 oz. can pumpkin
- ½ tsp ginger
- ½ tsp cloves
- 2 tsp baking powder
- 2 C. flour
- ½ tsp nutmeg

## Preparation

Mix first 3 ingredients together well.  Add pumpkin.  In separate bowl, mix remaining dry ingredients, add to wet ingredients. Mix and pour in greased pan.  Bake at 350 degrees for 25 to 30 minutes.
