categories: Breads, Rolls, & Doughnuts
---

## Ingredients

- (1 loaf)
- 1 C. sugar
- 1 ¼ C. cake flour
- ½ C. shortening
- ½ tsp salt
- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1 tsp baking soda
- 2 well beaten eggs

## Preparation

Mix all ingredients.  Pour into greased loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 45 min.  (Makes 1 loaf.)
