title: Honey Chicken
categories: Main Dishes
---

## Ingredients

- 3 T orange juice (or milk)
- 1 tsp. curry powder
- 3 T butter, melted
- 1 tsp. salt
- ¼ C. mustard
- 3 lb. chicken pcs.
- ½ C. honey

## Preparation

Mix first 6 ingredients together in sauce pan.  Pour over chicken pieces in a shallow roasting pan.

Bake at 350 degrees for about 1 hr., basting often.
