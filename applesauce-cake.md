## Ingredients

- 1 ½ C. sugar
- ½ tsp nutmeg
- 1 C. brown sugar
- 1 tsp lemon extract
- ¾ C. shortening {part butter}
- ½ C. molasses
- 2 lg. eggs
- 2 ½ C. applesauce
- 1 tsp cloves
- ½ C. raisins
- ½ tsp allspice
- 2 tsp soda
- 2 tsp cinnamon
- 3 ½ C. flour

## Preparation

Mix the first 10 ingredients, add applesauce, raisins, soda and flour.  Sprinkle top with crushed nuts and sugar.  Bake 325 degrees for 1 hr.  No frosting necessary.
