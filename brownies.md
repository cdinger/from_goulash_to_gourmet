## Ingredients

- 4 squares chocolate
- 4 tsp. vanilla
- 1 ½ C.  shortening
- 1 C.  nuts
- 2 ½ C.  sugar
- 2 C.  sifted flour
- 4 eggs, well beaten

## Preparation

Melt chocolate and shortening together.  Beat eggs with sugar.  Combine rest of ingredients.  Bake at 375 degrees in 9 x 13 pan, about 20 minutes (until shiny on top.)
